Project: ULT

Project: ULT is a 2D top down RPG game made to have an enticing story that makes you feel like your playing an anime that's made out of pixels.

Project: ULT is based off games like Undertale and Stardew Valley. It's story has 2 sides were one is undisclosed at least for this game.

In this game you will follow Riku and her story of revenge against the Adir of the 5 Realms a God/Demon Lord who had supposedly killed her family.
